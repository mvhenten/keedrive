Privacy Policy

Effective: May 26, 2026

What KeeDrive Is

KeeDrive is a browser-based KeePass (.kdbx) viewer that runs entirely in your browser. There is no backend server. No analytics. No cookies. No third-party tracking.

What Data We Access

KeeDrive requests the Google OAuth scope https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.file. This scope grants access only to files you explicitly open with KeeDrive—never your entire Drive.

When you choose "Open with → KeeDrive" from Google Drive, the app downloads the selected .kdbx file into your browser's memory and decrypts it locally using the password and key file you provide.

What Happens to Your Data

  • Your password and decrypted vault contents never leave your browser. All decryption happens client-side using kdbxweb.
  • The OAuth access token is held in memory only and discarded when you close the tab.
  • No data is transmitted to the developer, stored on any server, or shared with third parties.
  • The vault auto-locks after 5 minutes of inactivity; decrypted data is cleared from memory.
